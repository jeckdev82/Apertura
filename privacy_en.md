# Privacy Policy for Apertura

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the `Apertura` app processes users' personal data.

## 1. Data Controller

Data Controller:
`Gurini Pier Giacomo`

Contact email:
`jeckdev82@gmail.com`

## 2. Data We Process

The app only processes the data necessary for its operation.

In particular, the following data may be processed:

- content created or imported by the user, such as screenshots, images, graphic elements, and project data;
- local app preferences, such as language settings, appearance, export folders, and backup folders;
- technical information related to the status of Pro in-app purchases;
- data strictly necessary to manage purchases, restore purchases, and verify subscriptions through RevenueCat and Apple.

## 3. Purposes of Processing

Data is processed for the following purposes:

- to allow users to create, edit, save, and export projects;
- to enable the import of files and images voluntarily selected by the user;
- to save local preferences and local backups;
- to manage in-app purchases, Pro feature activation, and purchase restoration;
- to ensure the proper technical functioning of the app.

## 4. Where Data Is Stored

Project contents and imported files are stored locally on the user's device or in folders selected by the user.

The app does not use its own servers to upload or synchronize user projects online.

## 5. In-App Purchases

To manage purchases, subscriptions, Pro status, and purchase restoration, the app uses `RevenueCat`, integrated with Apple's payment systems.

When a user makes or restores a purchase, certain technical data related to the transaction and subscription status may be processed by RevenueCat and Apple in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Useful links:

- RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## 6. No Advertising and No Profiling

The app:

- does not display advertising;
- does not use advertising SDKs;
- does not perform commercial profiling of users;
- does not use tracking for marketing purposes.

## 7. Analytics and Monitoring

At this time, the app does not use analytics services, behavioral statistics tools, or user tracking tools for marketing purposes.

## 8. Permissions and File Access

The app can only access files and folders voluntarily selected by the user, for example to:

- import screenshots or images;
- export generated files;
- save local backups in a folder chosen by the user.

## 9. Legal Basis

Data processing is carried out:

- for the performance of the service requested by the user;
- to fulfill contractual obligations related to the purchase of Pro features;
- for the legitimate interest of the data controller in ensuring the security, stability, and proper functioning of the app.

## 10. Data Retention

Data stored locally remains on the user's device or in folders selected by the user until it is deleted by the user or until the app is uninstalled, except for any local backups voluntarily created by the user.

Any data processed by RevenueCat or Apple is retained according to their respective policies.

## 11. Sharing with Third Parties

Data is not sold to third parties.

Data may only be shared with providers strictly necessary for delivering in-app purchase services, in particular:

- RevenueCat;
- Apple.

## 12. User Rights

To the extent provided by applicable law, users may request:

- access to their data;
- rectification or updating;
- deletion;
- restriction of processing;
- objection, where applicable;
- to lodge a complaint with the competent authority.

For requests relating to data directly processed by the data controller, users may contact the reference listed in the "Data Controller" section.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time. If material changes are made, the updated version will be made available together with the app or through the project's official channels.
